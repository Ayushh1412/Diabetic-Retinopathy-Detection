Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

AI-powered Detection to Help diagnose Diabetic Retinopathy


What is Diabetic Retinopathy?

Diabetic Retinopathy is a diabetes-related eye disease that can cause vision loss. Early detection is crucial for preventing blindness. Our system uses advanced AI and image processing techniques to detect signs of diabetic retinopathy from retinal images.


How It Works

Our AI-powered system analyzes images of the retina, looking for signs of diabetic retinopathy. Once detected, the severity of the disease is classified to help doctors make more informed decisions about patient care.


Get Started

Upload an image of the retina to our system to get an instant diagnosis and the severity level of diabetic retinopathy.
